JULY

by

The Show Continues (in January)

July 2022, I (Junker), was hospitalized for two weeks. After struggling with anorexia for over a year, my immune system finally caved in. I caught a pneumonia, which should've resolved itself, but instead landed me with a tube in my back, incapable of doing anything. This was also while we were recording Sheldon's Passing, which we had to put on hold. During my stay, I had a lot of time to think and write about my life, the changes I had to make, the things I was grateful for and the visions being on morphine made me have.
